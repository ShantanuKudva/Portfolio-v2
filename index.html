    My Philosophy about Photography and Music.

    Photography for me has been a revealation for me since i got my first smartphone just before the lockdown was imposed.

    For me, Photography is not just an act of clicking a picture that has good composition. It serves as a double function for me to paint a picture in my head and get a suitable imagery for the story i create in my head at that moment of me clicking a picture
    Of course i wouldn't be writing them down and the pictures i clicked would be in my gallery merely for the composition
    But also, we can build different stories off the same picture long after i have forgotten the original backstory.

    The other fascinating thing that i find is Music. I can't emphasis enough on how beautiful this skill of creating a melody and writing down verses has such an impact in our though processes and sometimes just melody and the different types of music that you listen to, can give you that ideal kickstart for you to get a perfect daydream and support that daydreaming too.

    In a mood to experience how it feels like the world is ending in front of you and you are the lone survivor, Listen to Lacrimosa by Mozart.
    Wanna experience the soft touch of stars and imagine that you are sleeping outside in your soft lawn with full moon light on your face, you guessed it, Listen to Moonlight Sonata by Beethoveen.

    You get the point now.

    But yes, the two most amazing skills a person can possess is the skill of painting a picture with their photos and helping us build our sky-castles through music.
